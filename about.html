About Me

I'm a web developer based out of Marietta, GA. Currently, I am finishing my final semester at Kennesaw State University. I'll be graduating in December 2020 with a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology and a concentration in Mobile & Web Development. After graduation, I plan to relocate to Seattle, WA, and seek work in web development on a professional level.

My love for web development started in 2012 when I joined Tumblr. Many of the accounts I followed were creating and sharing custom themes. The default theme Tumblr provided was unappealing, but none of the custom layouts I found were exactly what I wanted. I taught myself HTML and CSS in order to personalize the custom themes I used, and eventually started building my own. In addition, I have taught myself vanilla JavaScript and jQuery.

I took a small hiatus to focus on my school work, and in that time I defined my design style and settled on a brand identity. In January 2020, I revisited my previous work, scrapping the projects that I felt no longer represented my abilities. For the projects that remain, I have been reimagining them and re-building them from the ground up.

When I'm not coding or focused on school, I can be found gaming and listening to music with friends and volunteering at a local cat shelter.

I pride myself on always being true to my word, genuine, and ambitious.
